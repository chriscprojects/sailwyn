Security

Designed around least-privilege access.

Sailwyn processes non-PII business, Brand Analytics, advertising, inventory, listing, and financial reporting data. Write actions are limited to advertising budget and bid updates and sending Amazon's standard review requests for eligible orders — each requires explicit seller approval and is fully audit-logged. Buyer names, phone numbers, addresses, shipping labels, and tax invoice data are not part of the product scope.

Token protection

Authorization tokens are stored encrypted and are never shown in the user interface, reports, or logs.

Tenant separation

Seller accounts, advertising profiles, sync jobs, reports, and operational logs are separated by account.

Auditability

Account access, authorization changes, sync activity, and every budget or bid change are logged with account, timestamp, actor, and result details.

Data use limitation

Data from an authorized Amazon account is used only to provide Sailwyn to that account. Sailwyn does not pool or aggregate data across sellers, create cross-seller benchmarks, provide one seller's data or insights to another seller, publish or share insights about Amazon's business, or use Amazon-derived insights for OneCommerce's own business purposes.
