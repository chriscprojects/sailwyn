Details

Start with a one-month free trial.

No credit card required. Cancel anytime.

  • The three plans above are our complete price list. Every customer pays the published price for the plan they choose, and we do not offer negotiated or quote-based pricing.
  • All prices are in US dollars and billed monthly. There are no setup fees, no onboarding charges, and we never take a percentage of your ad spend.
  • If you reach a limit on your plan we will contact you about moving up. We never bill overages automatically.
  • A connected Amazon account means one authorized Seller Central selling account and its associated Amazon Ads profiles for the supported marketplace.
  • Amazon limits how far back advertising history can be loaded when you first connect: 95 days for Sponsored Products and 60 days for Sponsored Brands and Sponsored Display. Your reporting history builds from there.
  • Sailwyn supports the Amazon United States marketplace.
  • Brand Analytics reports are available only to authorized sellers who meet Amazon's applicable eligibility requirements, including Brand Registry enrollment where required, and only for the brands, ASINs, and marketplaces available to that seller account.
  • Onboarding is handled directly by our team. Email chris.c@sailwyn.com to start your free trial.
  • Sailwyn uses each seller's Amazon data only to provide the Service to that seller. We do not pool or aggregate data across customers, create cross-seller benchmarks, publish or share insights about Amazon's business, sell Amazon data, or use Amazon-derived insights for OneCommerce's own business purposes.
